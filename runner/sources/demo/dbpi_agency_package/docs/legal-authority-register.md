# City of Red Vale DBPI Legal Authority Register

## Purpose

This register identifies the legal authorities, delegated powers, and controlling instruments under which the Department of Building Permits and Inspections operates. It is intended as the Department's canonical internal reference for legal provenance.

## Provenance Rules

1. Every adverse enforcement document should cite at least one ordinance or state-law authority.
2. Every permit decision should be traceable to intake, review, fee, and applicable technical-code authority.
3. Every records response should identify the controlling disclosure authority and any exemption basis.
4. Every workflow rule used by systems should be traceable to one or more authority IDs in this register.
